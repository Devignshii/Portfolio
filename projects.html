The Journey of Designing bhatnbhat.in

Understanding the Vision

Designing bhatnbhat.in started with understanding the client's vision—a YouTuber specializing in village and antique-styled cooking videos. The goal was to create a website that reflects the rustic charm and authenticity of traditional cooking, while being engaging and accessible to a modern audience.

Crafting the Design

I chose a green and brown color palette to evoke the natural elements and warmth of village life. The design needed to be simple yet elegant, ensuring that the focus remained on the content—delicious recipes and cooking videos. I crafted a clean, intuitive layout that guides visitors effortlessly through the site.
